# WidgetPro

*Last researched: 2025-01-10*

## Overview

WidgetPro is a project management tool targeting mid-market teams (50-500 employees). Series B funded ($45M), launched 2021. Primary competitor in our collaboration segment.

## Pricing

- **Free**: Up to 5 users, basic features
- **Pro**: $12/user/month — collaboration, integrations
- **Enterprise**: $25/user/month — SSO, audit logs, SLA

## Strengths

- Real-time collaboration is polished, launched Q4 2024
- Strong mobile experience (4.6 stars on App Store)
- Fast iteration speed — shipping weekly
- Good developer documentation for integrations

## Weaknesses

- No self-hosted option (enterprise blocker for some)
- Limited reporting/analytics compared to us
- Resource allocation is basic (our differentiator)
- Customer support response times declining (per G2 reviews)
